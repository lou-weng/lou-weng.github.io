♦ About Me ♦


How would I describe myself in one word? I would choose "explorer". After living in Toronto for the majority of my life, I decided to take the unprecedented step of moving to Vancouver for university. I've always been open to change and taking risks, but I will admit moving across the country was slightly extreme for me. However, I have come to admire and embrace the diverse people and experiences that have impacted me. Looking forward to the future, I am excited to see what is in store for me.

Career-wise, my interests lie in tech. I love learning about egnabling business and entrepreneurship through technology. You will often find me learning about new programming concepts and creating my own applications.

In my spare time, I am an avid enthusiast of Ultimate Frisbee and a total foodie. Please feel free to do some exploring yourself and look through the site. You might also find a video of me during a ramen-eating competition (where I placed in the top 25% percentile).
